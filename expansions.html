Marvel Dice Throne Missions

Marvel Dice Throne Missions adds cooperative PvE gameplay to the Marvel Dice Throne universe. Players select any hero and work together to complete mission objectives, facing thematic challenges and Marvel-themed villain waves. Players can earn "perks" through completing missions, granting powerful upgrades to assist in later, more difficult, missions.

The expansion includes mission cards, event mechanics, and team-focused gameplay. Success depends on understanding each hero's strengths, coordinating attacks, and managing resources to achieve the mission objectives.
