Welcome to Dice Throne

Dice Throne is a fast-paced card and dice battler built around hero identity. Each character has their own dice faces, offensive abilities, defensive rolls, and upgrade path, which makes matchups feel personal instead of generic.

This site is designed to help new players understand the flow of the game while giving fans a cleaner place to compare heroes, learn strategy basics, and jump into the bigger universe.

Gameplay

Learn the flow of a turn

Dice Throne turns are easy to follow once you understand the rhythm: upkeep, income, main phase, offensive rolls, defense, and cleanup. The trick is learning when to push for damage and when to spend combat points for setup.

Heroes

Every hero changes the puzzle

Some heroes are straightforward damage dealers. Others are timing-heavy control characters, sustain specialists, or tricky combo engines. Learning which style fits you is one of the best parts of the game.

The roster page collects the heroes into one cleaner reference so you can browse styles, strengths, and release sets without digging through product pages.

Strategy

Small decisions add up fast

Good Dice Throne play is about more than landing a flashy ability. You are managing rerolls, hand resources, combat points, and the tempo of the matchup every turn.

Co-op Expansion

Dice Throne Adventures

Adventures turns the duel system into a cooperative dungeon crawl with portal maps, minions, bosses, loot, and longer-form progression. It is the best bridge from competitive play into campaign-style sessions.
