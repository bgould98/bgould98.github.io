About

What this portfolio is for

This portfolio is a central place to keep track of my school projects and the improvements I make to them over time. It gives me one homepage where I can organize work from different classes and show how those projects develop.

Instead of leaving projects isolated in separate folders or assignments, I can use this site to collect them in one place and document the work I have completed.
