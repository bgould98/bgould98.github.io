Dice throne is a face-paced, tactical card and dice game of dueling heroes. Choose your hero, roll dice to activate unique abilities, and battle your way to victory. This site will guide you through the basics, strategies, and expansions of the game. --> Dice Throne Store <--

The Gameplay

Dice Throne is played in turns, where each player rolls their hero's dice to generate resources and trigger abilities. Players use these abilities to attack, defend, or manipulate the game state. Matches involve managing health, status effects, combat points, and strategic choices to outplay opponents. The game's structure combines dice randomness with tactical decision-making, giving each hero a unique flow and playstyle. --> To Gameplay <--

"Dice Throne can be played as a 1vs1 dueling game, in teams, and yes even cooperatively. Dice Throne now features 16 heroes to choose from, each featuring wildly different aesthetics and play styles." - Dice Throne About

What Are Heroes?

Heroes are the heart of Dice Throne. Each hero comes with their own unique dice, abilities, and playstyle, making every match feel different. Some heroes focus on powerful attacks, while others rely on defense, healing, or special mechanics. Learning your hero's strengths & mastering their abilities is the key to victory. --> To Heroes <--

What Are Some Strategies?

Mastering Dice Throne requires more than rolling dice; it's about planning and adapting each turn. Players must balance offense and defense, anticipate their opponents moves, and manage resources efficiently. Understanding each hero's strengths, optimal ability combos, and situational tactics gives you the edge in every match. Strategic positioning, timing your abilities, and exploiting opponent weaknesses are key to consistently winning battles. --> To Strategies <--

Dice Throne Adventures - Expansion

Dice Throne Adventures transforms the competitive Dice Throne experience into a cooperative dungeon crawl. Players embark on a campaign where they explore modular maps, battle minions, and face off against challenging bosses. This expansion introduces two main modes: the Portal Crawl, a tile-laying adventure with evolving scenarios, and the Boss Battle, a cooperative showdown against formidable opponents. It enhances replayability with legacy elements, evolving challenges, and the opportunity to use heroes from Seasons 1 and 2 in a new context. --> To Expansions <--

"Exploration, gold, treasure, upgrades, minions, boss battles, and even a shopkeeper named Rosella? Welcome to the latest addition to the Dice Throne universe!" - Dice Throne Adventures | Features

Marvel Dice Throne Missions - Expansion

Marvel Dice Throne Missions brings the Marvel universe into the Dice Throne arena. Players can team up with iconic heroes like Spider-Man, Thor, Loki, and Scarlet Witch to tackle solo or cooperative missions against infamous villains such as Ultron & Green Goblin. The expansion offers a variety of missions, each with unique scenarios and boss battles, providing a rich narrative experience. It also includes a one-shot mode for quick play sessions, allowing fans to immerse themselves in the Marvel universe with the familiar Dice Throne mechanics. --> To Expansions <--
