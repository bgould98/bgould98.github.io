Roster Guide

Choose a hero that matches how you want to win.

Dice Throne heroes are more than cosmetic reskins. Each one changes the tempo of the match, the decisions you make during rerolls, and how aggressively or patiently you want to play.

How to read the roster

  • Role: the hero's overall style, such as burst damage, control, sustain, or support.
  • Primary strengths: what that hero tends to do best once the match gets going.
  • Set: where the hero comes from, including core seasons, Marvel, X-Men, Outcasts, and special releases.

Good starting points

  • Barbarian, Pyromancer, and Gunslinger are approachable if you want clean, readable turns.
  • Tactician, Vampire Lord, and Cursed Pirate reward players who enjoy deeper resource and timing puzzles.
  • When in doubt, try a hero with flexible offense and defense before committing to a narrow gimmick.
