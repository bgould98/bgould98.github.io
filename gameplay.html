Getting Started & Turn Order

Quick setup

  • Choose a hero and set up their hero board, tokens, and dice.
  • Set starting Health and CP as shown on your hero leaflet.
  • Draw your starting hand, shuffle your deck, and roll to determine the Start Player.

(See official Rulepop guide here): RULEPOP

Complete turn order

  1. Upkeep Phase - Resolve start-of-turn status effects and passive abilities.
  2. Income Phase - Gain 1 CP and draw 1 card. (Start Player skips their first Income Phase.)
  3. Main Phase (1) - Play Hero Upgrades, Action cards, or sell cards for CP.
  4. Offensive Roll Phase - Perform up to 3 roll attempts, then activate an offensive ability.
  5. Targeting & Resolve - Determine the attack target and resolve the attack.
  6. Defensive Roll Phase - Defender(s) roll to activate their Defensive Ability.
  7. Main Phase (2) - Same as Main Phase (1); a final chance to play cards or upgrades.
  8. Discard Phase - Sell cards until you meet the hand limit (selling gives CP).

Tips for new players

  • Focus on learning one hero's dice and abilities first.
  • Manage your CP. Upgrades and cards can swing the game.
  • Play cards before and after rolls in the two Main Phases.
  • When you're done rolling, say “Ready to attack!” so everyone's clear.
